# 🌱 ReLife – AI Resource Rescue Network

## 📌 Overview

ReLife is an AI-powered resource donation platform that helps reduce waste by connecting donors with NGOs, schools, orphanages, and individuals in need. The platform enables users to donate reusable items such as books, clothes, furniture, electronics, school supplies, toys, and household essentials, ensuring that valuable resources reach those who need them instead of ending up in landfills.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Millions of reusable items are discarded every year while many communities struggle to access basic resources. Existing donation systems are often unorganized, making it difficult for donors and NGOs to connect efficiently.

---

## 💡 Solution

ReLife provides a smart web platform where users can easily donate reusable items. AI assists by categorizing donations, generating donation summaries, recommending suitable NGOs, and simplifying the donation process.
